Engineering Departments

Computer Science and Engineering

The Computer Science and Engineering department is equipped with the latest computers and software to facilitate hands-on learning and research. The lab is open to all students and faculty members.

Mechanical Engineering

The Mechanical Engineering department has state-of-the-art labs for design, manufacturing, and maintenance of machinery. The lab is equipped with tools and machinery to give students a practical and innovative approach to learning.

Electrical and Electronics Engineering

The Electrical and Electronics Engineering department has labs for power generation, transmission and utilization. The lab is equipped with advanced tools and equipment to promote sustainable and ethical practices.

Information Technology

Information technology is building communications networks for a company, safeguarding data and information, creating and administering databases, helping employees troubleshoot problems with their computers or mobile devices, or doing a range of other work to ensure the efficiency and security of business information .

Civil Engineering

Civil engineering is a professional engineering discipline that deals with the design, construction, and maintenance of the physical and naturally built environment, including public works such as roads, bridges, canals, dams, airports, sewage systems, pipelines, structural components of buildings, and railways.

Library

The college library is a hub for students and faculty members to access academic resources, research material, and other books. The library is equipped with computers, printers and other resources to support students and faculty members in their academic endeavors.
